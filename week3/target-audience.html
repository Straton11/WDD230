208 Sports

All Things Fitness

Target Audience

208 Sports is for anyone who is looking to get out there and be fit. It is ment for all ages. Make for an easy way for anyone to find fun things to do to keep fit.

Personas

  • Steve (27)

    • A single guy who loves playing basketball any chance he gets. He is always looking for the next place to go play. He works at amazon and loves to spend time with his friends. They are always talking about sports. He loves meeting new people and is open to networking to find new ideas of fitness.

  • Jenny (17)

    • High school soccer player. Has lots of friends that love spending time outside kicking the soccer ball around. They are constantly looking for new ways to play. She also loves playing other sports. Her and her family love going hiking on the weekend. She is most happy when she is being active.

Scenarios

  1. A family comes to Idaho Falls on vaction and is looking to do something active. They ask the front desk lady at the hotel if she knows of anything and she tells them to look up 208 Sports because they have a lot of fun ideas.

  2. A couple dads a looking for a place to play some basketball. They look up 208 Sports and find that there is a group that plays at the rec center everyday at 8pm. They end up going and have an awesome time.

  3. High school girls are looking to improve their soccer skills. They look up 208 Sports and see that there is an advanced league that is going to start in a couple weeks. They sign up and their skills improve rapidly.

  4. A couple moms are wanting to get back in shape. They decide that they want to join others who walk each day but can not find anyone else who wants to walk everyday. They look up 208 Sports and find a group of other moms that walk every morning.

  5. A group of co workers want to join a baseball league. But not everyone is a great baseball player so they are trying to find an entry level league. They get on 208 Sports and find the exact league they are looking for.
