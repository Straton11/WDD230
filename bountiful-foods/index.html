Carlsbad is a coastal city in the North County region of San Diego County, California, United States. The city is 87 miles (140 km) south of downtown Los Angeles and 35 miles (56 km) north of downtown San Diego. Carlsbad is known for its oceanfront beaches, but also has one of the most popular kids' parks in the country. Also in the city, you'll find a rich culinary tradition with a wide range of foods and wines, which you might want to try on a food tour or when cruising across town on a rented electric scooter.
